What does my BMI mean?

The body mass index (BMI) is a measure that uses your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy. The BMI calculation divides an adult's weight in kilograms by their height in metres squared.

Underweight (BMI < 18.5)

Healthy Foods

  • Nut butters, avocados, whole milk, cheese, eggs, lean meats, oily fish, whole grains, dried fruits, potatoes.

Meal Plan Example

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with whole milk, banana, and peanut butter.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken sandwich with cheese, avocado, and whole grain bread.
  • Dinner: Salmon, brown rice, and roasted vegetables.
  • Snacks: Trail mix, Greek yogurt, smoothies with fruit and nut butter.

Workout Plan (to gain weight)

  • Focus on strength training: squats, deadlifts, bench press, rows (3-4x/week).
  • Limit cardio to light sessions (1-2x/week).
  • Progressively increase weights and calories.

Normal Weight (BMI 18.5 - 24.9)

Healthy Foods

  • Lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, low-fat dairy.

Meal Plan Example

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with berries and granola.
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with grilled chicken and mixed veggies.
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with brown rice and broccoli.
  • Snacks: Fresh fruit, almonds, hummus with carrots.

Workout Plan (to maintain weight)

  • Mix of cardio (running, cycling, swimming) and strength training (2-3x/week each).
  • Flexibility and mobility exercises (yoga, stretching).

Overweight (BMI 25 - 29.9)

Healthy Foods

  • Leafy greens, lean meats, legumes, whole grains, berries, low-fat dairy, healthy fats (olive oil, nuts).

Meal Plan Example

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and tomatoes.
  • Lunch: Turkey wrap with whole wheat tortilla and veggies.
  • Dinner: Grilled fish, quinoa, and steamed broccoli.
  • Snacks: Apple slices, cottage cheese, raw nuts.

Workout Plan (to lose weight)

  • Cardio: Brisk walking, cycling, or swimming (30-45 min, 4-5x/week).
  • Strength training: Full body (2-3x/week).
  • Increase daily activity (take stairs, walk more).

Obese (BMI 30+)

Healthy Foods

  • Non-starchy vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains in moderation, legumes, berries, healthy fats in small amounts.

Meal Plan Example

  • Breakfast: Veggie omelet with tomatoes and spinach.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette.
  • Dinner: Baked cod, sweet potato, and green beans.
  • Snacks: Celery with hummus, low-fat yogurt, berries.

Workout Plan (to lose weight)

  • Low-impact cardio: Walking, swimming, cycling (start with 20-30 min, 3-5x/week).
  • Strength training: Bodyweight exercises (2x/week).
  • Gradually increase intensity and duration as fitness improves.
